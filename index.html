From the farm to your cup
coffee beans are the pits inside of harvested coffee cherries
the pits are separated from the fruits and dried with one of several methods
the raw green coffee is roasted, breaking down its cell structure and developing complex flavors
the roasted beans are ground and brewed using one of many methods such as pour over
